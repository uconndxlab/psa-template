The Sole Source Justification Questionnaire is to be completed for all non-grant funded Personal Service Agreements.

The sole source justification required must include the unique features of the service requested.

  • If this service is available from other vendors you must tell us what is unique about the service offered by the requested vendor as compared to other vendors. Provide a list of all of the other providers of similar services that you have evaluated and indicate the features that do not measure up to the requested service.

To insure the timely processing of your request, please be thorough. The larger the dollar value, the more detailed the justification needs to be.

Please complete the following form and return to Leslie Cote in Purchasing, Unit 6076.
