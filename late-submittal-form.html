PSA - Late Submittal Form

NOTE: This form must accompany all PSAs that are not submitted for approval prior to the start of contracted services.

Submitting a State contract for approval after the start of the contract period is a clear violation of State Contracting Policies and Procedures. During State audit reviews the University has been cited for such violations. Therefore it is extremely important that all departments submit PSAs for approval prior to the start date of the contract period.

* * *

PSAs valued at $3,000 or greater must be submitted at least 60 business days prior to the start date fo the contract.

PSAs valued at less than $3,000 must be submitted at least 30 business days prior to the start date of the contract

Contracts without grant funding must be submitted for approval to: Office of the Vice President and Chief Operating Officer, U-2014.

Contracts with grant funding must be submitted for approval to: Office of Sponsored Programs, U-4133.
